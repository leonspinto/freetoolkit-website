Free QR Code Generator

A QR code (Quick Response code) is a two-dimensional barcode capable of storing massive amounts of data such as URLs, raw text strings, or digital business cards. It is an industry standard used globally by major smartphones.

By utilizing this HTML5 Canvas powered QR Generator, the encoding engine parses your raw characters and renders the unique barcode square perfectly locally in your web browser. No external API is queried, guaranteeing that the links you convert remain entirely anonymous and private.
