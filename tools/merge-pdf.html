How to Merge PDF Files

Merging multiple PDF documents into a single, cohesive file has never been easier. Follow these simple steps to combine your files instantly:

  1. Upload your files: Click the "Select Files" button or drag and drop multiple PDF documents into the drop zone above.
  2. Reorder your pages: Once your PDFs are loaded, you will see visual thumbnail previews of the first page of each document. You can easily click, drag, and drop these thumbnails to arrange the files in the exact order you want them merged.
  3. Merge and Download: Click the "Merge PDFs" button. Within milliseconds, your new combined document will be generated and ready for download.

Benefits of Using This Tool

Our Free Merge PDF tool operates on a completely different architecture than most online converters. Traditionally, you would have to upload your highly sensitive files—like tax returns, employee contracts, or bank statements—to an unknown remote server, wait for it to process, and then download it.

Instead, FreeToolKit uses an advanced WebAssembly library called pdf-lib combined with Javascript to perform the entire merging operation locally on your device. Because your files never leave your browser:

  • Absolute Security: There is zero risk of data interception or server-side leaks because no servers are involved in the processing.
  • Instantaneous Speeds: You don't have to wait for large documents to upload via slow internet connections. Processing happens at the speed of your computer's CPU.
  • No Limits: Merge as many documents as your device's memory can handle, completely free of charge.
