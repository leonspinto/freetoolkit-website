How to Reorder PDF Pages

Have you ever scanned a large document only to realize some pages are out of order? The FreeToolKit Reorder PDF tool makes it incredibly simple to organize your document without needing expensive desktop software.

  1. Upload your PDF: Drag and drop your document into the upload zone. Our application will immediately generate a thumbnail for every page.
  2. Drag and Drop: Click and hold any page thumbnail, then drag it to its correct position. You can also delete unwanted pages using the small 'X' button on each thumbnail.
  3. Save and Download: Once everything is in right order, click the Save button. We will generate the reorganized PDF directly on your device instantly.
