How to Fix Upside-Down PDF Pages

It is very common to scan documents on a flatbed scanner only to realize half the pages were fed in upside-down or sideways. With the FreeToolKit Rotate PDF tool, repairing the orientation formatting is easy, visual, and highly secure.

  1. Load Document: Choose your PDF file. The browser will instantly load an interactive thumbnail view of every single page.
  2. Rotate Visually: Click the "Rotate 90°" button below the specific pages that are misaligned. The preview will spin to reflect the final product. You can also rotate the entire document identically.
  3. Secure Download: Click the Export button. The PDF geometry will be rewritten by processing exactly on your local computer, meaning you don't compromise any confidential text.
