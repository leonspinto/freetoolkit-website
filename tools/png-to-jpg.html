How to Convert PNG to JPG

PNG (Portable Network Graphics) files are excellent for maintaining complex alpha-channels (transparency features) for logos and UI elements. However, they are uncompressed by default, frequently causing massive file sizes that ruin server storage quotas and SEO Web Vital metrics.

Local Execution Privacy Layer

When you utilize an internet file converter, most scripts command your computer to physically upload your original PNG to their server. They utilize backend utilities like Python Pillow or FFmpeg to transcode it, and send the result back down to you.

This constitutes an unnecessary privacy risk, especially involving internal company diagrams or personal photographs. The FreeToolKit converter never touches a server.
