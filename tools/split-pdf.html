How to Split a PDF

Often, you will receive massive PDF reports or e-books where you only need to extract one or two specific pages for a presentation or email attachment. Instead of sending a cumbersome 50-page file to a client, you can use our splitting utility to isolate exactly what you need.

  1. Upload the Master Document: Drop your large PDF into the interactive zone. Our Javascript engine will automatically parse the file structure using PDF.js.
  2. Visually Select Pages: A grid of thumbnails will appear representing every page in your file. Click on the thumbnails you want to keep. They will highlight in purple.
  3. Extract: Click the extract button. A new, smaller PDF document containing only your selected pages will instantly generate for downloading.
