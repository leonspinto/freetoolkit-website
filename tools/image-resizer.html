How to Resize an Image Online

Platforms like Instagram, YouTube, and corporate HR portals frequently demand exact pixel dimensions for uploads (such as 1280x720 or 500x500). Instead of guessing, use this tool to artificially clamp your photos perfectly.

  1. Upload photo: Drag and drop your source file into the upload zone.
  2. Enter new dimensions: Type in your desired width or height. By default, the 'Lock Aspect Ratio' switch will calculate the opposing edge mathematically so your photo does not appear stretched or squashed.
  3. Download constraints: Click the green button to initiate the Canvas rendering operation natively in your system. It is instantaneous and requires zero server uploads.
