Why Convert JPG to PNG?

JPG (or JPEG) is highly optimized for complex photographs but often suffers from artifacting on harsh text boundaries or graphics. PNGs natively retain maximum data through lossless rendering algorithms.

Although converting a JPG to a PNG will not retroactively inject transparent background channels (since JPG inherently lacks alpha channels), moving into PNG format is the required first step before utilizing other web development tooling platforms focusing on high graphical fidelity.
