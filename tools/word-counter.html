How to Use the Free Word Counter

Whether you are a student writing an essay, a marketer drafting a tweet within character limits, or an SEO specialist optimizing meta descriptions, knowing your exact string metrics is critical.

  1. Paste Your Text: Copy your document from Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or your IDE and paste it into the editor above.
  2. Instant Analysis: Our Javascript algorithm automatically calculates the word count, character count (including spaces), sentence density, and paragraph breaks.
  3. 100% Secure: Because this is an entirely client-side web application, your text is never uploaded to a database or sent across a network. It is entirely private.
