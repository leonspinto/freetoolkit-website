Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

At FreeToolKit, your privacy is our primary concern. Because of the architecture of our platform, protecting your data isn't just a policy—it's a technical guarantee.

Client-Side Processing

All logic, conversions, merging, and rendering done on FreeToolKit happens entirely inside your local browser. We do not have a backend server meant to process documents or images. When you use tools like our PDF Merger or Image Compressor, your files never leave your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, view, or store the files you manipulate using our tools.
  • We do not require account registration or collect personal identities.

Analytics and Advertising

We use standard web analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to understand aggregated traffic patterns (e.g. tracking page views, which tools are most popular, and general geographical metrics). We use advertising networks (like Google AdSense) to support the ongoing development of this free resource. These third-party services may use cookies as defined by their respective privacy policies.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact Form.
