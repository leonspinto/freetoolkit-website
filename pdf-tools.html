Why use our PDF Tools?

Our PDF tools run on a modern WebAssembly library called PDF-lib. This means instead of uploading your sensitive tax documents, contracts, or personal records to a remote server for processing, your browser does all the heavy lifting locally.

This allows for instantaneous downloads and guarantees that your files are 100% secure since they never leave your local computer or mobile device.
